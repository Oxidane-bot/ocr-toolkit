b. Foster a culture of psychological safety
c. Reduce team size for better communication
d. Focus on individual contributions

b. Mandate completion with strict deadlines
c. Reduce the duration of the training
d. Replace diversity training with general HR workshops

a. Offer targeted career development programmes
b. Increase wages for all positions
c. Outsource recruitment processes
d. Eliminate performance reviews

a. Implement flexible working hours
b. Increase supervisory roles
c. Focus on strict attendance policies
d. Limit employee feedback sessions

a. Introduce collaborative project management tools

b. Restrict virtual team sizes
c. Encourage more frequent team meetings
d. Reduce reliance on digital communication

c. Focus solely on senior-level decisions
